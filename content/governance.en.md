title: "Governance"
---

The collective is organized into working groups that aim to achieve specific
objectives validated by the group. Each working group is autonomous and
regularly presents its progress and seeks validation from the collective
according to a consent decision-making process. Certain decisions are made by
consensus (inclusion of members, long-term financial issues, and
ethical/fundamental issues).

Following the adoption of the charter, work was carried out to refine the
governance and legal structure of the organization.
